A continent in motion

Functional landscapes

Natural processes play a vital role in shaping landscapes and ecosystems. Such natural processes include flooding, weather conditions, natural calamities, natural grazing, predation and scavenging. Natural processes lead to what we call “functional ecological landscapes”.

Enabling natural processes

Rewilding Europe aims to create enabling conditions for wilder nature across Europe and has started this process at selected priority landscapes and pilot sites. Within our now nine rewilding landscapes we promote natural processes through various kinds of agreements with landowners, local park and reserve authorities, area managers and concession holders. We have established dozens of rewilding agreements, with now numerous pilots and initiatives demonstrating rewilding.
